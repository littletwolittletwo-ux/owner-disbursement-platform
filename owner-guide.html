1. Welcome

Thank you for partnering with LiveLuxe to manage your property. This guide explains exactly how your monthly disbursement (payout) is calculated, what deductions are made, and when you can expect to receive your funds.

We believe in full transparency. Every line item on your monthly statement is explained in this document so you always know where your money goes.

What Is a Disbursement?

A disbursement is your monthly payout from LiveLuxe. It represents your share of the rental income earned from your property, after all applicable fees, commissions, and expenses have been deducted.

What You Receive Each Month

  • A bank transfer — your net payout, deposited directly into your nominated bank account

2. The Rules — When You Get Paid

The Two Conditions

For a booking to appear on your monthly disbursement, both of these must be true:

1 The booking has finished — the guest checked out on or before the last day of that month.

2 The payout was received — the platform payment arrived in the LiveLuxe trust account during that month.

If either condition is not met, the booking simply rolls to the next month. No money is lost.

Even though the booking starts and finishes in May (checkout Saturday May 31), the payout doesn’t arrive until June. This booking would appear on your June statement instead.

The Critical Pattern

When End-of-Month Bookings Roll Forward

• Airbnb check-in on Friday/Saturday/Sunday at month end → payout lands Monday (next month)

• Booking.com checkout on Saturday/Sunday/Monday at month end → payout lands Friday (possibly next month)

This is rare and only affects 1–3 bookings per month at most. The money is never lost — it always appears on the following month’s statement.

7. Understanding Each Fee

Channel Commission (16.5% / 13.5%)

Charged by the booking platform (Airbnb, Booking.com, or VRBO) — not by LiveLuxe. The platform takes their commission before sending payment to the trust account. You never see this money arrive; it’s already deducted.

Cleaning Fee

After each guest, your property is professionally cleaned. The cleaning fee is charged to the guest as part of the booking and then paid to the cleaning team. This is an owner cost — deducted after the management fee has been calculated on the channel payout.

Typical monthly cleaning costs range from $600–$750 depending on the number of bookings and property size.

Management Fee (18% + GST)*

LiveLuxe’s fee for full-service property management, including:

  • 24/7 guest communications and support
  • Listing optimisation and dynamic pricing
  • Booking management across all platforms
  • Check-in/check-out coordination
  • Property inspections and maintenance
  • Monthly financial reporting and disbursements

Software Fee ($65.99/month per property)

A flat monthly fee covering the technology platforms that manage your listing:

  • KeyNest — secure key management for guest access
  • PriceLabs — dynamic pricing to maximise your revenue
  • Enso — guest experience and communication platform

Charged once per property per month, regardless of bookings.

Property Expenses

Bills or costs paid on your behalf, passed through at cost with no markup:

  • Owners Corporation (OC) levies
  • Strata / body corporate
  • Water bills
  • Electricity
  • Internet
  • Repairs, photography, other
Important: Expenses are deducted after the management fee. They do not affect the management fee calculation — they are simply subtracted from your remaining balance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a booking I know about not on my statement?

It may not have qualified because either the guest hasn’t checked out yet, or the platform payment hasn’t arrived in trust. It will appear on a future statement once both conditions are met.

Why does the gross differ from what I see on Airbnb?

Airbnb shows you their “host payout” (after their commission). Our statement shows the full guest payment as the gross, then deducts the channel fee as a separate line. Both are correct.

Can I change my bank details?

Yes. Contact LiveLuxe at least 5 business days before the next disbursement.

Is GST included in the management fee?

GST is shown separately. The management fee is 18% of channel payout. GST (10%) is applied on top. Total effective rate: 19.8%. Note that management fee rates may vary per owner agreement — some agreements include GST in the rate, and commission percentages may differ.
